Prompt Library

Copy-ready prompts to help you complete and extend this session's work.

PROMPT 1
AI shopping reconnaissance
I want to understand how my business appears in AI shopping results.
Prompt 
I want to understand how my business appears in AI shopping results.

My product/service: [describe what you sell - be specific]
My target customer: [who buys it - role, need, or situation]
My price range: [approximate pricing]

Please help me with two things:
1. Write 5 search queries a customer might use to find what I sell - use their language, not mine. Include both short keyword searches and longer conversational searches.
2. Tell me what information AI shopping engines typically pull from product listings when generating results, and what I should prioritize having on my product pages.
PROMPT 2
Audit your product data for AI visibility
I want to audit my product listing for AI shopping visibility.
Prompt 
I want to audit my product listing for AI shopping visibility.

Here is my current product description: [paste your existing product description]

Please review it and tell me:
1. What key information is missing that an AI shopping engine would need to recommend this product? (specifications, use cases, pricing clarity, reviews, comparisons)
2. What questions would a customer ask an AI before buying this product, and does my current description answer those questions?
3. Rewrite the product description optimized for AI discovery - keep my voice but add the missing structured information.
PROMPT 3
Competitive AI shopping analysis
I searched for "[your category search]" in [ChatGPT/Perplexity] and these competitors appeared: [list the ones that showed up].
Prompt 
I searched for "[your category search]" in [ChatGPT/Perplexity] and these competitors appeared: [list the ones that showed up].

I want to understand why they appeared and I did not.

Please help me:
1. What do these types of businesses typically have on their product pages or listings that makes them discoverable in AI shopping?
2. What is the likely reason a business in my category would not appear in these results?
3. What are the top 3 changes I could make to my product page or catalog to become more visible in AI shopping searches?

My business: [brief description of what you sell and where you sell it]
PROMPT 4
Set up for Shopify + ChatGPT integration
I want to connect my Shopify store to ChatGPT so customers can discover and buy my products inside AI conversations.
Prompt 
I want to connect my Shopify store to ChatGPT so customers can discover and buy my products inside AI conversations.

My products: [describe your product catalog - types, price range, number of SKUs]
My current Shopify situation: [do you have a store, or are you starting one]

Please give me:
1. A step-by-step checklist of what I need to set up - from Shopify account to ChatGPT merchant connection
2. What product data should I clean up or add before connecting, to maximize visibility?
3. What should my product descriptions focus on so an AI shopping agent can accurately recommend my products in a conversational search?
PROMPT 5
GEO strategy for a product or service
I want to build a GEO strategy so my [product/service] appears in AI-generated shopping results.
Prompt 
I want to build a GEO strategy so my [product/service] appears in AI-generated shopping results.

Product/service: [describe it]
Target customer: [who you are selling to and what problem they are solving]
Current presence: [where you are listed today - Shopify, Etsy, your own website, Amazon, etc.]
Main competitors: [1-3 competitors who are ahead of you]

Please create a GEO action plan with:
1. The top 5 questions my target customer would ask an AI before buying, and how my product should answer each one
2. Schema markup and structured data I should add to my product pages
3. A content plan for FAQ pages, comparison pages, or review content that AI engines would index
4. One quick win I can do this week
