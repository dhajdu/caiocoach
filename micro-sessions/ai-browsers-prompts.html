Prompt Library

Copy-ready prompts to help you complete and extend this session's work.

PROMPT 1
Competitive Research Brief
Track what competitors are doing across pricing, features, and news
Prompt 
I need a competitive brief on [Competitor A], [Competitor B], and [Competitor C].

For each company, find:
- Current pricing (entry and mid tier if applicable)
- Any product or feature updates in the last 30 days
- Recent press coverage or announcements
- Current promotions or free trial offers

Compile the results in a simple comparison I can share with my [sales / product / leadership] team. Flag anything that looks like a significant change from what I might have seen 30 days ago.
PROMPT 2
Industry News Briefing
Stay current on a topic without spending 30 minutes reading tabs
Prompt 
Give me a briefing on what happened in [industry or topic - e.g., AI regulation, fintech in Southeast Asia, supply chain logistics] in the last [7 days / 2 weeks / month].

Focus on:
- The 3 to 5 most significant developments
- What changed and why it matters
- Any signals that could affect [my role / my company / my clients]

Keep it under 400 words. Include source links for anything I should read in full.
PROMPT 3
Travel or Event Research Package
Plan a trip or event without building a spreadsheet from scratch
Prompt 
I need to plan a [business trip / team offsite / conference attendance] to [city or location].

Details:
- Dates: [start date] to [end date]
- Budget: [accommodation budget per night]
- Priority: [near city center / airport / specific area]
- Preferences: [any loyalty program or specific requirements]

Find me:
- 3 accommodation options with current pricing and availability
- The fastest route from [home city] including flight options and approximate cost
- [Optional: 2 venue options for a team dinner]

Compile the results as a simple summary I can share with [my assistant / my manager / my team] to get approval.
PROMPT 4
Supplier or Vendor Shortlist
Research vendors or service providers before a buying decision
Prompt 
I need to build a shortlist of [type of vendor or service - e.g., payroll software, marketing agencies, cloud storage providers] for a company of [size] in [industry or region].

For each option you find, note:
- Company name and website
- What they specialize in or are known for
- Approximate pricing or pricing model (free trial, per user, custom)
- One user review or reputation signal (positive or critical)

I need [3 to 5] options. Prioritize [value for money / enterprise features / ease of setup / regional support in [country]].
PROMPT 5
Page Explainer (Sidecar Mode)
Ask questions about the page you are currently reading without copy-pasting
Prompt 
I am on [this page / this article / this document].

Explain the key argument in plain language.
What is the author's main claim?
What evidence do they use to support it?
What are the 2 or 3 things I should remember from this?
Is there anything here I should fact-check before I share it?
