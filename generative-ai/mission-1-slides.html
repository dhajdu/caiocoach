Brief 2

The 6 Beliefs of an AI Officer

BELIEF 1
Accuracy follows clarity
AI performs at the level of your thinking, not its own.
BELIEF 2
AI is like a Buddy that learns you
Pick one model. Build context. Mastery beats variety.
BELIEF 3
Frameworks turn chaos into clarity
Don't guess. Design. AI rewards structured thinkers.
BELIEF 4
Information is the new creativity
AI runs on context, not vibes. Order in, wisdom out.
BELIEF 5
Curiosity drives mastery
Try things. Break things. Progress favors the curious.
BELIEF 6
AI needs a human in the loop
AI scales knowledge. You set direction. Be the orchestrator.

Brief 3

AI doesn't understand you.

  • !
    It predicts, not thinksAI finds patterns in data and predicts what comes next. That's it. No understanding. No memory. No judgment.
  • !
    Every session starts from zeroAI has no context unless you bring it. You own the continuity.
  • !
    It can hallucinateAI can be confidently, completely wrong. The human stays in the loop to catch it.
"But with the right context, AI performs like it does understand you. That's your job to provide."

Brief 4

Three levels. Same model. Different results.

  • 1
    Bare promptGeneric. Could be about anything. You'd never send it.
  • 2
    RACE frameworkSpecific. On-brand. You could edit and send it.
  • 3
    AI InterviewSounds like it came from inside the company. Because your answers made it yours.

Say: "I need help with [task]. Before you start, ask me 3–4 questions." AI finds gaps you didn't know existed.
