Prompt Library

Copy-ready prompts to help you complete and extend this session's work.

PROMPT 1
GEO Rewrite
Use this to turn any existing piece of content into a GEO-optimized version.
Prompt 
I want to rewrite this content for GEO (Generative Engine Optimization). Apply the E-E-A-T framework: add specific experience, demonstrate expertise with concrete examples, and make the writing trustworthy and citable by AI models. Keep the rewritten version to 80-120 words. Make each paragraph a clear, standalone answer that an AI could quote directly. Here is the original content: [PASTE YOUR CONTENT]
PROMPT 2
GEO Audit
Use this to get a fast diagnosis of any webpage or post before you rewrite it.
Prompt 
Analyze this content for GEO performance. Identify: (1) which E-E-A-T signals are present and which are missing, (2) whether the content is written in answerable units of 80-120 words, (3) whether claims are specific and verifiable or vague and general, and (4) whether any citations or credible sources are referenced. Give me a short verdict for each and a priority list of what to fix first. Here is the content: [PASTE YOUR CONTENT]
PROMPT 3
Write From Scratch for GEO
Use this when starting a new piece of content and you want it GEO-ready from the beginning.
Prompt 
Write a [blog post / LinkedIn post / website bio / product description] about [TOPIC]. Use GEO best practices: write in answerable units of 80-120 words each, include specific examples and data where possible, build in E-E-A-T signals (firsthand experience, demonstrated expertise, credible citations, and a clear author voice), and make every paragraph quotable as a standalone answer. The target audience is [DESCRIBE YOUR AUDIENCE]. The key point I want to make is [YOUR MAIN IDEA].
PROMPT 4
Find Citable Sources
Use this to find credible sources to strengthen your content before publishing.
Prompt 
I am writing content about [TOPIC]. I need 3-5 credible, citable sources that AI models would treat as high-authority. Prioritize academic research, published studies from universities, and data from established research organizations. For each source, give me the organization, a one-sentence description of what the research shows, and why it strengthens the E-E-A-T signals in my content.
PROMPT 5
Turn a Long Post Into Answerable Units
Use this to restructure dense writing into GEO-friendly chunks.
Prompt 
I have a long piece of content that needs to be restructured for GEO. Break it into individual sections of 80-120 words each. Each section should answer one specific question or make one clear point. Add a short heading to each section written as a question that someone might type into a search engine or AI tool. Keep all the original information but make the structure easier for AI models to scan and cite. Here is the content: [PASTE YOUR CONTENT]
