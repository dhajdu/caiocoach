Build Your AI Team with Paperclip

Agentic AI Essentials · Hands-on micro-session

Session Objective

The bottleneck in most teams is not capability - it is capacity. There is only one of you. You can work faster, or you can work smarter. But there is a third option: build a team that works while you do the part only you can do.

Grading Criteria
Real goal
Is the goal something the student is actually working toward? A strong goal is specific, time-bound, and requires multiple types of work. A weak goal is generic or hypothetical.
Team design
