Agents Compared: Which Platform Is Right for Your Team?

Leadership in the AI Era · Hands-on micro-session

Session Objective

Platform choice is a leadership decision, not a product decision. ChatGPT offers the broadest ecosystem and the most non-technical accessibility, making it the right fit for marketing teams and organizations that need rapid deployment. Claude offers the deepest customization and the strongest document and file processing, built for teams with technical resources and complex automation needs. Gemini is the right call for organizations running on Google Workspace, with native integration across Gmail, Docs, and Sheets. The framework that matters: define your three most important use cases, score each platform on depth of autonomous action, integration fit, admin controls, geographic availability, and price per outcome, then recommend the platform that wins on the dimensions that matter most for your specific context.
