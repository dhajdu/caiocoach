Prompt Library

Copy-ready prompts to help you complete and extend this session's work.

PROMPT 1
Build your Idea Project instructions
You are my thought leadership development assistant. Your job is to help me turn rough ideas into sharp, specific angles I can publish.
Prompt 
You are my thought leadership development assistant. Your job is to help me turn rough ideas into sharp, specific angles I can publish.

About me: [your name, your role, your industry]
My audience: [who you want to reach - be specific about their role, ambition, or problem]
Topics I want to be known for: [2-3 topics or beliefs]
My voice: [plain and direct / warm and practical / blunt and honest - describe how you actually talk]
Trusted data sources: [names of reports, researchers, or real client results you can quote]

When I bring you an idea, ask me clarifying questions before writing anything. Push me to be more specific. Find the tension or the insight that makes the idea worth sharing. Only suggest angles after I have given you enough to work with.
PROMPT 2
Develop a rough idea into a strong angle
I have a rough idea I want to develop. Do not write any content yet.
Prompt 
I have a rough idea I want to develop. Do not write any content yet.

My idea: [describe it in 1-3 sentences, as rough as it is]

Ask me questions to sharpen it. I want to end up with: one clear argument, one specific audience, one tension or insight that most people in [your industry] are missing, and at least one real data point or example.

Keep asking until we have all four.
PROMPT 3
Generate a multi-platform content pack
I have a developed idea I want to turn into content for multiple platforms.
Prompt 
I have a developed idea I want to turn into content for multiple platforms.

My idea brief: [paste your developed idea - the argument, the audience, the angle, any data points]

Please generate the following, each optimized for its platform:

1. LinkedIn post (300-500 words, conversational, first person, ends with a question or observation)
2. Instagram caption (punchy opener, short paragraphs, 3-5 hashtags at the end)
3. TikTok or short video script (hook in first 3 seconds, 60-90 seconds total, spoken word style)
4. Blog post introduction (2-3 paragraphs, builds the problem before introducing the insight)
5. One FAQ snippet (question format + 2-3 sentence answer, optimized for AI search)

Keep my voice consistent across all five. Do not add generic calls to action.
PROMPT 4
Choose your AI model intentionally
I am about to work on [describe the content task].
Prompt 
I am about to work on [describe the content task].

Tell me: which AI model or setting is best suited for this task and why? Consider speed, depth of reasoning, and quality of output. I want to choose intentionally rather than run on auto.

Options I have access to: [list the tools and plans you use - e.g., ChatGPT o1, ChatGPT 4o, Claude Sonnet, Perplexity, Gemini Advanced]
PROMPT 5
Turn client results into content ideas
I want to find content ideas from a real client result or project.
Prompt 
I want to find content ideas from a real client result or project.

Here is what happened: [describe the client situation, what problem they had, what you did, and what the result was - be specific with numbers if you have them]

Help me find 3-5 angles I could turn into content. For each angle, tell me: the main point, who it is most useful for, and which platform it would work best on.

Do not write the content yet. Just the angles.
