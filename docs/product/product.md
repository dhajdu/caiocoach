# Product Vision — CAIO Coach

**Boundary:** CAIO Coach (caiocoach.com) is the founder's AI leadership coaching brand within AI Officer LLC. Sister sites: ai-officer.com (cohort events + membership), davehajdu.com (personal/writing).

---

## What it is

A content-led marketing site for Dave Hajdu's coaching practice. Senior leaders wrestling with AI in their organisation come here to:

1. Read the founder's writing on AI leadership.
2. Browse the *Infinite Leverage Blueprint* — a 14-part narrative on building a one-person agentic company.
3. Download blueprints across four offices — Leverage, Talent, Operations, Innovation.
4. Book a coaching session with the founder via the inquiry form.
5. Subscribe to the 50/50 Memo.

The site is mostly content. Conversion goes through one form: the coaching-signup form that lands in the shared CRM.

---

## Audiences

### 1. Senior leaders considering coaching

C-suite executives, founders, engineering managers, HR leaders. They came in via writing, a podcast, or referral. They want to evaluate whether direct coaching with the founder is worth $X. Their question: "Is this person credible, and is this a real practice?"

### 2. Memo subscribers

Operators who want a regular dose of leadership writing without booking a call. They bought into the founder's voice, not yet his services. The memo is the warming layer.

### 3. Self-serve readers

Practitioners who want frameworks they can apply themselves — the blueprints. They may never book; they're audience that compounds the brand.

---

## Values, ranked

1. **Founder voice.** Writing is the moat. The site lives or dies on whether the founder's perspective lands.
2. **Credibility cues.** Senior leaders need to see that this is a real practice, not a side project.
3. **Frictionless inquiry.** A senior buyer should be one click and one form from a coaching conversation.
4. **Reusable frameworks.** Blueprints turn readers into evangelists.

---

## Moat

- **Founder writing.** Volume × frequency × point of view. Hard to fake.
- **Cross-brand network.** Inquiries flow into the shared CRM with sister brands — coaching can convert into events or consulting.
- **Practical framework library.** Blueprints are downloadable and citable.

---

## Business model

| Channel | Pricing | Demand shape |
|---|---|---|
| Coaching | Bespoke | Inquiry-driven, low volume, high margin |
| Memo | Free | Compounds slowly; warms the audience |
| Blueprints | Free / linked checkout | Lead magnet → inquiry funnel |

Coaching is the revenue. Memo and blueprints are the audience-building layers that feed it.
