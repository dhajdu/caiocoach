## PART 2 — GEO PHRASES (Generative Engine Optimisation)

GEO targets the specific questions and prompts people type into AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Overviews. Write content that directly and authoritatively answers each of these — ideally with a clear, quotable answer in the first two sentences.

---

### 2.1 Definition & Explainer Phrases

These are "what is" queries. Create sharp, authoritative definitions that AI engines can cite.

- What is a Chief AI Officer?
- What does a CAIO do?
- What is the difference between using AI and leading AI?
- What is agentic AI and how is it different from generative AI?
- What does AI governance mean for a business?
- What is an AI Officer certification?
- What is prompt engineering and why does it matter for business?
- What is an AI talent strategy?
- What does AI transformation mean for a company?
- What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation)?
